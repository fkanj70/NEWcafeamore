Our Story

Where Every Guest Becomes Family

Café Amore was born from a simple belief: that a truly great cup of coffee is an act of love. Nestled in the heart of Orléans, our family-owned café is a place where Italian tradition meets warm Canadian hospitality.

We source the finest espresso beans, craft every drink by hand, and bake our pastries fresh each morning. From our silky cappuccinos to our legendary tiramisu, every item on our menu carries the same care we put into welcoming each guest through our door.

When you walk into Café Amore, you're not just a customer — you're part of our family. Pull up a chair, breathe in the aroma of fresh espresso, and let us take care of you.
